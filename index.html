Why Cybersecurity Matters?

Cybersecurity is vital in today's digital age as it protects individuals and organizations from the increasing risks of cyber threats. With the growing reliance on technology for communication, business, and daily activities, safeguarding sensitive information has become crucial to preventing data breaches, identity theft, and financial losses. In Sri Lanka, where digital adoption is rapidly expanding, the need for robust cybersecurity measures is more important than ever to combat phishing attacks, online scams, and other cybercrimes. Prioritizing cybersecurity ensures a safer digital environment, builds trust in online platforms, and empowers individuals to make informed and secure choices online.
